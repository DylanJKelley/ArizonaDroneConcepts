Our Mission

For those who want to transform their visual concepts into amazing UAV videography and photography footage, Arizona Drone Concepts is the smart choice for you. We provide incredible aerial footage and professionally edited videos with the latest software, useful for: search/rescue, security surveillance, construction, real estate, special events; you name it. We also specialize in agriculture and building or roof inspections by applying the newest technology in infrared cameras....

We take valuable assets and man power off of the ground and provide a perspective otherwise unreachable using modern techniques. No other service can match Arizona Drone Concepts knowledge of the sky, applying our combined ten years of UAV experience as certified Part 107 licensed pilots, as well as the experience of a 15-year tenured helicopter pilot. With the combination of Arizona Drone Concepts' unrivaled experience and the latest UAV technologies, the sky is truly the limit with what we can do for you.
